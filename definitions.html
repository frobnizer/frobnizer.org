Market Analytics

These indicators help traders assess market depth, fundamental value, and project health.

Trade Price

The market price as determined by the latest executed trade. Utility: Provides real-time entry/exit valuation.

Floor Value

The intrinsic value of each token if the liquidity pool were distributed across the entire supply. Formula: Burned Liquidity √∑ Public Float Utility: Establishes the fundamental price floor.

Daily Volume

The total value of buys and sells in a trailing 24-hour window. Utility: Measures market liquidity and interest levels.

Market Cap

The total market value of the project based on the latest trade. Formula: Trade Price √ó Public Float

Community Equity

The current market value of all tokens held outside the liquidity pool. Formula: Trade Price √ó Currently Held Utility: Represents the total wealth held by the community.

Burned Liquidity

The total USD value of the FBZR and WETH assets paired in the liquidity pool. Utility: Confirms the size and permanence of the trading environment.

Liquidity Coverage

The value of the liquidity pool divided by the community equity. Formula: Burned Liquidity √∑ Community Equity Utility: Measures the pool's ability to absorb community sell pressure.

Backing Ratio

The percentage of the trade price that is fundamentally supported by the floor value. Formula: (Floor Value √∑ Trade Price) √ó 100 Utility: Indicates price stability and "over-extension" relative to the floor.

Rolling Performance

The price change in a trailing 24-hour window, represented as a percentage. Utility: Tracks short-term momentum.

Transactions

The total number of buys and sells in a trailing 24-hour window. Utility: Reflects network engagement and frequency of activity.

On-Chain Specifications

Real-time data points derived directly from the Base blockchain reserves and token supply.

Public Float

The total supply of tokens that are either in the liquidity pool or held by the community. Utility: Defines the maximum supply potentially available for trading.

Currently Held

The number of tokens in circulation that are not in the liquidity pool. Formula: Public Float - Pooled Frobnizer

Pooled Frobnizer

The number of FBZR tokens currently locked in the liquidity pool. Utility: Monitors token scarcity within the automated market maker (AMM).

Pooled Ethereum

The amount of WETH paired with FBZR in the liquidity pool. Utility: Measures the available buy-side liquidity.
