Frobnizer

The quintessential token built on Base

The name chosen for this artifical intelligence crafted and vibe-coded altcoin is inspired by a placeholder term that originated in hacker culture. Created without a fanciful roadmap and free from predefined utility that never materializes, it explores the true ability of collective narrative. There is no central authority deciding the direction, and none of the supply is allocated to a team or treasury reserve. Any perceived value is not found in unnecessary over-engineering or vague promises for the future, but in what it has the potential to become organically through an engaged community. It is less a product idea than a fairly launched experiment.
